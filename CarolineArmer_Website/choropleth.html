eBIRD BIVARIATE CHOROPLETH MAP

For this map I wanted to see whether there are any interesting demographic trends among birders in my home state. I used eBird checklist submission data from 2010, as well as the percentage of each county's population living in urbanized areas. I normalized the checklist data by each county's population and created three classes for degree of urbanization and number of checklists submitted. I got a lot of help on creating a readable color scheme and legend design from this tutorial (thanks Joshua!).
