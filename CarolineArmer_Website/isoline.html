TEMPERATURE ISOLINE MAP

This map was made for an assignment in my introductory cartography class. Historic averages of maximum daily temperature for North American cities were used to interpolate the maximum temperature for the whole area of North America. I used ArcMap's interpolation and spline tools to generate the isolines, which represent 10-degree intervals of temperature. In Adobe Illustrator, I smoothed the isolines and added the text, figures, and weather icons.
